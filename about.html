Hi, my name is Kirill.

I'm a digital craftsman, living in St. Petersburg, Russia. Born and raised in Omsk (Siberia) in the middle of 2014 I found a new opportunity for myself in web development.

Several years ago I started to contribute to Ruby and Ruby on Rails projects & improve my knowledge in this stuff.

Currently I'm working as a software engineer at Epam, mostly focused on Ruby/Ruby on Rails & related technologies. Also I'm searching for interesting opportunities & working on my own projects. I very much enjoy solving different problems & I’m most happy when I’m creating meaningful experiences.

Recently, my interests are limited to the study of web technologies, but I like photography (especially analog one), music, my bike ridings and traveling. Sometimes I read books, or pass some online courses or take part on Hacktoberfest's variety of GitHub projects. Also I'm interesting in Elixir functional programming language & Phoenix framework.
